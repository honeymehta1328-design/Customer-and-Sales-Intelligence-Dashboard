# Customer-and-Sales-Intelligence-Dashboard

An interactive multi-page Power BI dashboard developed to analyze E-Commerce sales performance, customer behavior, profitability, and regional business insights. This project transforms raw business data into meaningful and actionable insights through advanced data visualization, KPI monitoring, and business intelligence reporting.

---

# Project Overview

The dashboard was designed to provide executive-level insights into sales trends, customer performance, product profitability, and regional revenue distribution. The project focuses on improving business decision-making through interactive reports and analytical storytelling using Power BI.

---

# Dashboard Pages

## 1. E-Commerce Performance Overview
- Executive-level KPI monitoring dashboard
- Analysis of Total Sales, Total Profit, Return Rate, and Total Orders
- Revenue growth trend analysis over time
- Category-wise revenue contribution analysis
- Product profitability insights
- Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering

---

## 2. Sales Performance Overview
- Advanced sales and profitability analysis dashboard
- Treemap visualization for product profit contribution
- Waterfall chart for profit contribution analysis
- Geographic sales analysis using map visuals
- Interactive filtering by Region, Segment, Category, Year, and Ship Mode
- Business-focused analytical reporting

---

## 3. Customer & Regional Insights
- Regional revenue performance analysis
- High-value customer identification
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Segment-wise sales achievement gauges
- Customer contribution and profitability insights
- Customer intelligence and regional performance tracking
